Who We Are

Smart Tiffin was born from a simple observation: while thousands of homemakers possess incredible culinary skills, millions of students and working professionals struggle to find healthy, affordable meals.

Founded in 2022, we set out to bridge this gap by creating a platform that connects talented home chefs with food lovers seeking authentic, home-cooked meals.

Our vision is to transform the way urban India eats by making home-cooked food accessible, affordable, and convenient while creating economic opportunities for homemakers.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving change through home-cooked meals

Our Mission

To provide fresh, hygienic, and delicious home-cooked meals to urban populations while empowering homemakers to earn sustainable incomes from their culinary skills.

We believe that everyone deserves access to healthy, affordable food, and every talented cook deserves recognition and financial independence.

Our Vision

To become India's leading platform connecting customers with authentic home-cooked meals, serving 100+ cities and empowering 10,000+ homemakers by 2027.

We envision a future where no one has to compromise on health due to busy schedules, and no homemaker's culinary talent goes unrecognized.

Empowering Homemakers

At Smart Tiffin, we believe in the power of economic independence. Our platform enables homemakers to monetize their culinary skills while maintaining the flexibility to work from home.

We provide training, kitchen hygiene certifications, and marketing support to help our partners succeed. Many of our home chefs have doubled their household income while doing what they love.

By creating these opportunities, we're not just delivering meals - we're transforming lives and strengthening communities.
